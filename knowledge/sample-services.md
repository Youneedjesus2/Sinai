## Personal Care Services

We provide in-home personal care assistance for adults who need support with daily activities. Services include:

- Bathing, grooming, and hygiene assistance
- Dressing and mobility support
- Meal preparation and feeding assistance
- Medication reminders (not administration)
- Light housekeeping and laundry
- Companionship and social engagement

**Who qualifies:** Adults aged 18 and older who have a documented need for personal care support. A care assessment is required before services begin.

**How to get started:** Contact us to schedule a free in-home assessment. Our care coordinator will work with you or your family to design a personalized care plan.

---

## Skilled Nursing Visits

Licensed nurses visit clients in their homes to provide health monitoring and care coordination. Services include:

- Vital signs monitoring (blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen levels)
- Wound care and dressing changes
- Post-surgical recovery support
- Chronic condition monitoring
- Coordination with physicians and specialists
- Medication management review

**Who qualifies:** Clients who have been referred by a physician or hospital discharge planner and have a documented clinical need for skilled nursing.

**Insurance:** Many skilled nursing visits are covered by Medicare, Medicaid, or private insurance. Contact us to verify your coverage.

## Companion and Homemaker Services

For clients who primarily need non-medical support and social connection:

- Grocery shopping and errand running
- Transportation to appointments
- Companionship visits and conversation
- Reading assistance
- Activity planning
- Monitoring for safety and changes in condition

**Who qualifies:** Any adult who needs support maintaining independence at home and does not currently require personal care or skilled nursing.

## Consultation Scheduling

To book a consultation, reply with your preferred date and time or ask to be connected with our scheduling team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you provide 24-hour care?**
A: Yes, we can arrange live-in or round-the-clock care for clients with complex needs. Contact us to discuss options.

**Q: How quickly can services start?**
A: In most cases, we can begin services within 48 to 72 hours of completing the intake assessment, depending on care plan requirements and caregiver availability.

**Q: Do you accept insurance?**
A: We accept Medicare, Medicaid, and many private insurance plans. We also offer private-pay options. Our team can help verify your benefits before services begin.

**Q: What if I need to change or cancel my appointment?**
A: Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule or cancel. We understand that circumstances change and will do our best to accommodate your needs.
